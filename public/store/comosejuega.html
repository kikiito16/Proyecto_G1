Player Mechanics & the Game.

Tired of been brutally beaten by the college life? Well, this is not gonna change, but at least you can take all this tense and self-hating for sleep 3 hours/day and play our game.

The mechanics of the game are simple, with your finger you can make the player moved on through each week.

The objective gain the money to compensate all the second enrollments that you have did on all this years, and go the exit to the next Week.

But be careful, you will have to fight and defend from a lot of enemies, that in a first touch you can think that they aren't what they seem to be.
